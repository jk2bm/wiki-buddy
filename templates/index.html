Ask wiki-buddy a question.

wiki-buddy will attempt to find the answer(s) on Wikipedia.

Some tips for using wiki-buddy:

1. Capitalizing proper nouns is optional, but highly recommended.

2. Rare acronyms, typos or too much question complexity may result in slow processing time and/or faulty answers.

3. wiki-buddy's knowledge is limited to information found on Wikipedia.

4. Certain forms of questions may not be supported; if wiki-buddy is unable to answer a question, try rewording it.
